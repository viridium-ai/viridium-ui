Scope 2 Emission

Scope 2 emission counts the CO2 and its equivalents due to the purchase of electricity, and/or other energy product for the consumption of the entity. The amount of electricity purchased needs to be segregated by location if the methodology used for emission calculation is based on where the electricity is distributed.

The following are the aspects to be considered when collecting data

  • Locations, or sites
  • Contracts with distributors
  • Amount of electricity by month, quarter, or year
